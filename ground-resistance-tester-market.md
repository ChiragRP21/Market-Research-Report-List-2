<p><p>Ground Resistance Tester Market Types</p><p>1. Grounding System Resistance Tester: This type of tester is used to measure the resistance between the grounding system and Earth. It ensures the effectiveness of the grounding system in dissipating electrical faults and preventing electrical shock.</p><p>2. Insulation Resistance Tester: This tester measures the resistance of insulation materials and determines if they are providing adequate protection against electrical leakage. It helps identify any potential safety hazards and ensures the integrity of electrical systems.</p><p>3. Earth Continuity Tester: This type of tester is used to measure the continuity of the earthing conductor. It verifies whether the earth connection is properly established and ensures the safety of electrical devices, preventing the risk of electric shock.</p><p>4. Current Leakage Tester: This tester detects any leakage of current from electrical equipment. It helps identify faulty or defective equipment, preventing electrical hazards and ensuring electrical safety in buildings.</p><p>5. Ground Bond Tester: This tester measures the resistance between the grounding conductor and any exposed conductive parts of electrical equipment. It ensures the integrity of the grounding system and prevents potential electrical hazards caused by faulty grounding connections.</p></p>
