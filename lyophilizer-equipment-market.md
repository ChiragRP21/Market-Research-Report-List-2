<p><p>Lyophilizer equipment, also known as freeze dryers, are used to remove water or solvents from frozen biological material, typically in the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and food industries. The process involves freezing the material and then sublimating the ice directly from solid to vapor, bypassing the liquid phase. The resulting product is stable and can be stored for long periods without degradation.</p><p>The lyophilizer equipment market is experiencing rapid growth, driven by several key factors.
