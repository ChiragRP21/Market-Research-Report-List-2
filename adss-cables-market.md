<p><p>ADSS (All-Dielectric Self-Supporting) cables are used in the telecommunications industry for aerial installations. There are two main types of ADSS cables available in the market: ADSS Flextube and ADSS Loose Tube. ADSS Flextube cables have a single, flexible gel-filled buffer tube, which houses the fibers, providing lightweight and easy installation options. On the other hand, ADSS Loose Tube cables have multiple gel-filled buffer tubes, offering enhanced protection against extreme weather conditions and potential damage. Both types of cables are designed to support themselves without the need for additional support systems.</p></p>
