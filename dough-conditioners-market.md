Powder dough conditioners are dry ingredients that can be easily mixed into the dough. Liquid dough conditioners are in liquid form and are added directly to the dough mixture. Paste dough conditioners have a thicker consistency and are added in measured amounts to the dough. Each type of dough conditioner provides different benefits and can be chosen based on specific baking requirements.</p></p>
