<p><p>OCR (Optical Character Recognition) Software is a technology that enables the conversion of scanned images or printed documents into editable and searchable data. OCR software has various applications in industries such as banking, healthcare, retail, education, and government.</p><p>The OCR software market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing need for data digitization, rising volumes of paper-based documents, and the growing adoption of automation technologies.
