<p><p>The phased array antenna market can be classified into two types: active phased array antennas and passive phased array antennas. Active phased array antennas consist of multiple individual radiating elements that are connected to independent phase shifters and amplifiers, allowing for beamforming and steering of the antenna's radiation pattern. In contrast, passive phased array antennas do not require active components and instead rely on the design and placement of the antenna elements to achieve beamforming and steering. Both types have their own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
