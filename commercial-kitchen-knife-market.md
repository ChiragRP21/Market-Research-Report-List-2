<p><p>The Commercial Kitchen Knife Market has two main types of products, namely Stamped Blade and Forged Blade. Stamped Blade products are made by cutting the blade shape out of a large sheet of steel, resulting in a lighter and thinner knife. On the other hand, Forged Blade products are made by heating and shaping a single piece of steel, resulting in a heavier and more durable knife. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the commercial kitchen industry.</p></p>
