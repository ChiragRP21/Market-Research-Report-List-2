<p><p>The aircraft heaters market consists of different types of heaters used in aircraft to provide warmth and maintain appropriate temperatures. Electric heating systems utilize electrical energy to generate heat and are commonly used in smaller aircraft. Exhaust shroud heaters transfer heat from the aircraft's engine exhaust to warm the cabin. Combustion heaters burn fuel to create heat, and they are used in larger aircraft. Additionally, there are other types of heaters available in the market, which may include infrared heaters or hot water heaters, with each providing unique heating capabilities for specific aircraft requirements.</p></p>
