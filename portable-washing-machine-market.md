<p><p>The portable washing machine market consists of two main types: top loader and front loader. Top loader types are designed with a vertical drum where the clothes are loaded from the top. They are generally smaller in size and lighter in weight, making them easier to transport. On the other hand, front loader types have a horizontal drum and a door on the front for loading clothes. They tend to have larger capacities and are often more energy-efficient than top loaders.</p></p>
