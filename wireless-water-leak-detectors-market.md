<p><p>The wireless water leak detectors market consists of two main types: passive leak detectors and active leak detectors. Passive leak detectors are devices that continuously monitor the surrounding area for any signs of water leakage. These detectors rely on proximity sensors or moisture-absorbing materials to detect leaks. On the other hand, active leak detectors are devices that actively detect water leaks by regularly emitting electronic signals and analyzing the reflections. They are typically more sophisticated and provide real-time alerts when a leak is detected.</p></p>
