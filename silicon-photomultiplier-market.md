<p><p>The Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM) market can be categorized into two types: Monocoque and Array. The Monocoque type refers to a SiPM device composed of a single unit without any integrated circuit or additional components. It provides accurate and sensitive photon detection. On the other hand, the Array type consists of multiple SiPMs, usually arranged in a square or rectangular shape, offering enhanced detection capabilities and higher signal-to-noise ratio. Array SiPMs are widely used in various applications requiring high-performance photon counting, imaging, and spectroscopy.</p></p>
