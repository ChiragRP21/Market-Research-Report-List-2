<p><p>The e-Nose market consists of various types such as QMB/SAW, conducting polymers, metal-oxide sensors, and others. QMB/SAW (Quartz Microbalance/Surface Acoustic Wave) sensors operate by detecting changes in mass or film properties on a quartz crystal surface. Conducting polymers are sensors that change their electrical conductivity when exposed to specific gases or volatile compounds. Metal-oxide sensors detect gases by measuring the change in electrical resistance when gases interact with metal-oxide films. The "others" category includes various types of e-Nose sensors like optical, gas chromatography, and bio-electronic nose sensors.</p></p>
