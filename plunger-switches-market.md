<p><p>Plunger switches are categorized into two main types in the market: single plunger switches and double plunger switches. Single plunger switches consist of a single plunger that moves up and down when activated. They are commonly used in applications where a simple on/off function is needed, such as in keyboards or calculators. On the other hand, double plunger switches have two plungers that work simultaneously to provide additional functionality, such as multiple switch positions or enhanced control options. These switches are typically used in more complex devices or machinery where multiple functions have to be performed.</p></p>
